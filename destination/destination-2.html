Overview

Jaipur is a fantastic destination for history buffs, architecture lovers, and anyone interested in India's rich culture and heritage. Known as the "Pink City," it's a must-visit in Rajasthan.

Adventure seekers and explorers in Jaipur can marvel at majestic forts, intricate palaces, and vibrant markets. For a more relaxed experience, Jaipur offers serene gardens, beautiful temples, and mouthwatering Rajasthani cuisine to enjoy.

If any of that sounds appealing, you'll love Jaipur and its welcoming atmosphere. Read through this complete Jaipur travel guide for more information on what to expect!

Where Is Jaipur?

First things first -- where is Jaipur on the world map?

Jaipur is a major city located in the state of Rajasthan in India (in South Asia). India is a large country known for its diverse geography, bordered by Pakistan, China, Nepal, Bhutan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar.

Jaipur is situated in the northwestern part of India, forming part of the "Golden Triangle" tourist circuit along with Delhi and Agra. The city is well-known for its royal history, colorful architecture, and unique desert landscape.
